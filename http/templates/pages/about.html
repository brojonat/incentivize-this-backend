About IncentivizeThis

Bounties for content creation

What is IncentivizeThis?

IncentivizeThis is a platform that bridges the gap between businesses looking for authentic promotion and content creators looking to monetize their work. We make it easy for businesses to create bounties for specific types of content, and for creators to claim those bounties by producing the requested content.

For Businesses

Get authentic, organic promotion for your brand across multiple platforms:

  1. 1

    Create a Bounty

    Describe what kind of content you want (e.g., "A Reddit post with at least 1k upvotes about our product"). Our AI helps you refine your requirements to ensure quality.

  2. 2

    Fund Your Bounty

    Set your reward amount and fund the bounty with USDC. Your funds are held securely in escrow until the content is verified.

  3. 3

    Review & Approve

    Creators submit their content for review. Our system verifies it meets your criteria, and upon approval, the creator is automatically paid.

For Creators

Monetize your content creation skills and grow your audience:

  1. 1

    Browse Bounties

    Find bounties that match your platform and content style. Filter by platform (Reddit, YouTube, Twitter, etc.) and reward amount.

  2. 2

    Create Content

    Make content that meets the bounty requirements. Be authentic and follow the platform's guidelines. Quality content performs better!

  3. 3

    Submit & Get Paid

    Submit your content URL and your USDC wallet address. Once verified, you'll receive your payment instantly via the Solana blockchain.

Supported Platforms

Create bounties and submit content across multiple platforms including Reddit, YouTube, Twitter, HackerNews, Instagram, Twitch, and more. Each platform has specific content verification criteria to ensure quality and authenticity.

Join Our Community

Have questions? Want to discuss bounty ideas? Join our Discord community to connect with other creators and businesses using IncentivizeThis.
